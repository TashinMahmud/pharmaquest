Bangladesh Quiz Questions and Answers

1. What is the most common antibiotic used in Bangladesh?
   a) Amoxicillin
   b) Ciprofloxacin
   c) Azithromycin
   d) Penicillin
   Answer: a) Amoxicillin

2. Which disease is most prevalent in Bangladesh?
   a) Malaria
   b) Dengue
   c) Tuberculosis
   d) HIV
   Answer: c) Tuberculosis

3. What is the national vaccination program called?
   a) EPI
   b) NIP
   c) VIP
   d) HPI
   Answer: a) EPI
