Japan Quiz Questions and Answers

1. Which medicine is commonly used for seasonal allergies in Japan?
   a) Cetirizine
   b) Ibuprofen
   c) Paracetamol
   d) Aspirin
   Answer: a) Cetirizine

2. What is a major public health concern in Japan?
   a) Obesity
   b) Aging population
   c) Malaria
   d) Dengue
   Answer: b) Aging population

3. Which vaccine is part of Japan's routine immunization?
   a) BCG
   b) HPV
   c) Rabies
   d) Yellow Fever
   Answer: b) HPV
