Sweden Quiz Questions and Answers

1. Which vitamin is commonly supplemented in Sweden due to long winters?
   a) Vitamin A
   b) Vitamin B12
   c) Vitamin D
   d) Vitamin C
   Answer: c) Vitamin D

2. What is the Swedish healthcare system known for?
   a) Universal coverage
   b) Private insurance
   c) No hospitals
   d) No vaccinations
   Answer: a) Universal coverage

3. Which disease is rare in Sweden?
   a) Diabetes
   b) Tuberculosis
   c) Flu
   d) Asthma
   Answer: b) Tuberculosis
