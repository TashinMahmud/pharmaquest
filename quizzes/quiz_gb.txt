England Quiz Questions and Answers

1. What is the name of the UK's public health system?
   a) NHS
   b) CDC
   c) WHO
   d) Red Cross
   Answer: a) NHS

2. Which disease was eradicated in the UK by vaccination?
   a) Smallpox
   b) Polio
   c) Measles
   d) Malaria
   Answer: a) Smallpox

3. Which medicine is commonly prescribed for pain in the UK?
   a) Paracetamol
   b) Ibuprofen
   c) Aspirin
   d) Codeine
   Answer: a) Paracetamol
