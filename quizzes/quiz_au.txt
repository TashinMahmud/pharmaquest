Australia Quiz Questions and Answers

1. Which animal is a common carrier of the Hendra virus in Australia?
   a) Kangaroo
   b) Fruit bat
   c) Koala
   d) Emu
   Answer: b) Fruit bat

2. What is the national immunization program called in Australia?
   a) AIP
   b) NIP
   c) VIP
   d) AussieVax
   Answer: b) NIP

3. Which disease is nearly eradicated in Australia?
   a) Polio
   b) Malaria
   c) Dengue
   d) Tuberculosis
   Answer: a) Polio
