Spain Quiz Questions and Answers

1. Which Mediterranean diet component is common in Spain?
   a) Olive oil
   b) Butter
   c) Coconut oil
   d) Lard
   Answer: a) Olive oil

2. What is a major public health concern in Spain?
   a) Obesity
   b) Malaria
   c) Yellow fever
   d) Rabies
   Answer: a) Obesity

3. Which vaccine is mandatory for children in Spain?
   a) HPV
   b) BCG
   c) MMR
   d) Rabies
   Answer: c) MMR
